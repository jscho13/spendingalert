9/22/2018

Many online banks offer much higher yields on savings accounts and they are FDIC insured up to $250,000. Plus having a savings account that is seperate from your local bank so you can automatically save money each month.



Check their ratings on Google and other websites to make sure they are a good bank. Rates change so check back here or at the website of the bank that you are comparing to make sure you are getting the best interest rates.
