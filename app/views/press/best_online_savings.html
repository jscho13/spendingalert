Best Online Savings Accounts with Highest Interest Rates

9/22/2018

Many online banks offer higher interest rates on savings accounts then local banks. A good idea is to have a savings account that automatically deducts money each month and is different from your local bank so you don't have easy access to the money.


Check the banks ratings to make sure they are a quality bank and also that you understand the terms and conditions. Make sure there aren't any special restrictions such as min balances to receive the advertised rates. Rates change so check back here or at the website of the bank that you are comparing to make sure you are getting the best interest rates. Always confirm that the bank you end up choosing is FDIC Insured. FDIC Lookup
